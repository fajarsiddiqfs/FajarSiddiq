My Digital Story

Till then I bootstrapped 3 startups that became profitable businesses with no funding, full time while self-employed. I've been travelling and doing remote work from my laptop since 2005 doing branding & marketing work for creative designs, product launches and web-development. I have helped local startup clients make a profit over $50k of sales in e-commerce, conference events, marketing campaigns and also successfully launched their products. I've been in the gig & passion economy and I enjoy doing contract work and also doing things I love. I continue to learn & grow my digital skills includes Content Creating, Newsletters, Podcasts, Community Building, Graphic Design, UI/UX Design, Business Development, Video-Editing, Video-Recording & Audio Recording, Social Media Advertising, Astrography, GPT-3, Marketplaces, JAMstack JavaScript, APIs, and Markup.

Self-Taught, Bootstrapped & Independent

Fajar Siddiq is the co-founder of Euphorya™, Singapore based Creative Design Agency which he started in his room in 2005. In 2011 he registered the business in Yishun, Singapore with his friends building a creative design agency offering graphic designs & web-design products to startups, communities & companies. Fajar is responsible for setting the overall direction and product strategy for the business. He leads as a graphic designer and web-developer of Euphorya’s branding & marketing services and development of the web design services. Fajar graduated in Architectural at the Institute Of Technical Education, Balestier Campus in 2003.

Fajar attended Institute Of Technical Education, Balestier and studied architecture before founding Euphorya. He started as a freelancer running a side project designing t-shirts, drawing vector illustrations of faces of people on photoshop and doing silk-screen printing t-shirts selling it online locally and worldwide. In his early life, he also picked-up yo-yoing as a hobby when he was 12 years old and Fajar also represents the pinnacle of yo-yo skill across the globe as professional yo-yo player, contest yo-yo judge and also as a yo-yo instructor.

In 2011, Fajar co-founded Dement Clothing™, a Singapore based clothing brand. The designer clothes made for street & lifestyle culture products from printed graphic designs on a t-shirt, polo-t, hoodie long sleeve. In 2014, Fajar co-founded Ilm Productions™ after returning from the Islamic holy city of Makkah Al-Mukarramah & Al-Madīnah Al-Munawwarah for the pilgrimage (Umrah) in Saudi Arabia, he founded a start-up team up with the dakwah community to do creative and media work like designing, video-recording/editing and live-streaming.
